About GEM

Golden Eagle Makers (GEM) strives to make a positive impact on the community by harnessing the diverse skill sets of students from various majors across campus. The club's mission is to facilitate community-based projects that are spearheaded by students, empowering them to create designs and manufacture them through hands-on experiences and teamwork. GEM seeks to foster a culture of inclusivity and collaboration, where students can explore and pursue their passions in engineering with like-minded peers.
