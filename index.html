About GEM

Golden Eagle Makers (GEM) is a student-run organization that uses engineering to serve the community. Our mission is to provide opportunities for students of all majors to explore their interests in engineering, while engaging in meaningful activities related to the field. GEM strives to foster a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members, and to create a sense of community among those who share a passion for engineering.
